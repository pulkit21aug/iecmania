graduation is not the end of the students relationship with the college.All of the former students automatically become members of the  Alumini Association .The association is run by a commitee of elected graduates and PG and exists to foster links between the college  & alumini .In this way alumini are part of  a social and professional network .

The Alumini  relation office assists the alumini. Association in carrying out its functions and is the central point for alumini activities, including reunions, lectures,receptions, overseas  gatherings and association events .Alumini can volunteer to oganize events , write articles  for the alumini magazine, sit on the Executive Committee or volunteer as contacts for alumini within geographical areas or countries
